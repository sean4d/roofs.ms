Your Google Business Profile is the single biggest local-SEO lever for a
roofer — it drives the Map Pack, the star rating, and most "roofer near me"
calls. The website supports it; the GBP is the engine. Work top to bottom;
the items are ordered by impact.

(`src/config/site.ts`) — keep them **identical** everywhere (NAP consistency
is a ranking factor).

## 5. Reviews (highest-ROI ongoing task)

- [ ] **Respond to every review** — good and bad, within a day or two. Owner
      responses are a ranking and trust signal. Thank people by name; reference
      the job.
- [ ] **Ask every happy customer** using your direct link:
      **https://g.page/r/CZ5YiwvqNReNEBM/review** (also now on your site's
      reviews page). Text it the day the job wraps — that's when satisfaction
      peaks.
- [ ] **Never** offer discounts/gifts for reviews, and never review your own
      business — both violate Google policy and can wipe your reviews.
- [ ] Aim for **steady velocity** (a few new reviews every month) rather than a
      one-time burst — Google rewards consistency.

## 6. Photos & video (post real work, regularly)

- [ ] Upload your **47 real job photos** in batches over time (not all at once)
      — fresh photos signal an active business.
- [ ] Cover the categories: **Exterior**, **At work** (crews on roofs),
      **Team**, **Logo**, **Cover photo**, plus before/after storm shots.
- [ ] Add **1–2 new photos per week** from recent jobs. Name files
      descriptively before upload (e.g. `roof-replacement-gulfport-ms.jpg`).
- [ ] Add a short **video** (even a phone walkthrough of a finished roof) —
      profiles with video get more engagement.

- [ ] Every post: 1 photo + 1–2 sentences + a button (Call or "Learn more" →

## 8. Q&A, messaging, booking

- [ ] **Seed the Q&A** yourself: post 4–6 common questions (e.g. "Do you help

- [ ] **Website/booking link** — point the profile's action button at
      `/free-inspection`.
- [ ] Optional: use a tracked website URL (UTM: `?utm_source=gbp`) so you can
      see in analytics how many leads the profile drives.
